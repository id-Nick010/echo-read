# Echo Read

 **Echo Read is your hands-free reading companion.** Whether you're multitasking, easing into a story, or just too tired to read, Echo Read turns written text into spoken word with full control. Navigate sentence by sentence or paragraph by paragraph, follow along with real-time highlighting, and pause, rewind, or skip ahead whenever you need. It’s reading without the eye strain and with way more chill.
